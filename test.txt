日本獣医師会は、アジア地域の獣医師会や研究機関との緊密な連携の下、最高水準の学術プログラムと、心のこもったおもてなしを提供させていただく所存です。皆様お一人おひとりのご参加を、心よりお待ち申し上げております。

本大会の成功に尽力いただいたAVMAの皆様、そして全ての関係者の方々に深く感謝申し上げ、私の挨拶とさせていただきます。ありがとうございました。」

##英語##
-蔵内挨拶
"President of the WVA, President of the AVMA, distinguished guests, and veterinary professionals from around the world. I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude for these five days of productive discussions and exchanges.

Over these five days, we have engaged in extensive discussions ranging from infectious disease control to clinical veterinary medicine, wildlife medicine, and the application of AI. The sharing of crucial insights on global challenges, particularly regarding zoonotic and transboundary diseases, disaster veterinary medicine, and the international standardization of veterinary education, has been a significant achievement.

At WVAC2026, to be held at the Tokyo International Forum from April 21st to 24th next year, we will carry forward these discussions under the theme 'One Health for a better Tomorrow: Veterinary Medicine Holds the key.' We aim to take new steps toward realizing a sustainable society through veterinary medicine.

The Japan Veterinary Medical Association, in close collaboration with veterinary associations and research institutions across Asia, is committed to providing the highest standard of academic programs and heartfelt hospitality. We sincerely look forward to welcoming each and every one of you.

I would like to conclude by expressing my deep gratitude to the AVMA and all those who contributed to the success of this congress. Thank you very much."
